NELSON MANDELA

The Man Who Fought For Equality

Nelson Mandela was the first black president of South Africa, elected after time in prison for his anti-apartheid work

Here's a time line of Nelson Mandela's life:

  • 1942: Mandela joined the African National Congress

  • 1952 to 1955: Mandela directed peaceful, nonviolent acts of defiance against the South African government and its racist policies

  • 1994: Presidency of South Africa

  • 1999: Nobel Peace Prize Winner
